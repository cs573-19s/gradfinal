Experiment

In this experiment, the participant will be presented with a randomly generated graph of 2 data points. There will be 10 square area trials, 10 line length trials, 10 brightness trials, and 10 velocity trials.
The participant will be shown the value of the first data point. They then must estimate the value of the second point, between 1-100. All entries should be whole numbers (no decimals or fractions).
